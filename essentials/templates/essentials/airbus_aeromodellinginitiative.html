Airbus Aeromodelling Initiative

It’s an honour for Pravega to host the Aero-modelling initiative in association with our Title Sponsor Airbus. Because science should not be exclusive, this is a joint venture to introduce underprivileged primary school students to the wonder of Aero-modelling, Planes✈ and the science of things that fly. Launched in 2018, it's a fun, interactive session taught by Airbus employees to kindle the flame of science in these kids.

About

Battle it out with the finest in the country, debate on the up and coming scientific topics, with the sharpest logic and the most articulate English.

Are you up for the challenge?

Prizes

The Winner of the gets the Airbus A380 Prize of INR 4,000 and the runner up gets the Airbus A350 Prize of INR 2,000.

Guidelines
  • The speakers will be judged on their vocabulary, voice modulation, pronunciation, clear conveyance of ideas, quality of arguments and answers and their adherence to the time limit.
  • No unparliamentary language will be allowed.
  • Registrations closes on 16th January.
Prelims
  • Speakers get to choose their stances (for/against).
  • The topic shall be made public 24 hours before the debate.
  • Each speaker gets 2 minutes to present their arguments, followed by interjections (1 questions) by the judge. A question must be answered in under 45 seconds.
  • After the prelims, the best speakers will move to the finals.
  • The results of the prelims will be declared at 9PM. The topic for the finals, to be held the next day, will also be made known at that time.
Finals
Each speaker gets 5 minutes to present their arguments, followed by interjections (2 questions). A question must be answered in under 3 minutes.

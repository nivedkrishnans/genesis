About

Express. Inspire. Mesmerise. Bewilder. Confound.

This event invites entries under 1500 words, spin poignant poems, sombre musings, guffaw inducing tales or narrate striking experiences subject to our given constraints.

Guidelines
  • One entry per participant will be allowed. Please note that this must strictly be an original work by the participant.
  • The story must be strictly below 1500 words, adhering to the prompt and constraints given.
  • Special focus towards coherence of the piece and clarity in content will be exercised during the judging process.
  • Constraints to be announced shortly (CHECK FOR UPDATES ON THE WEBSITE!)

Guidelines

  • Following the release of prompt and opening of submission portal, the entries may be submitted online through the portal post registration, till January 10th, 2019, 11:59 PM.
  • Late submissions will NOT be considered at ANY cost.
  • The decision of the panel of judges is binding in terms of choice of the winners.

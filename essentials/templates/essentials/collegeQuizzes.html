College Quizzes

Pravega is proud to present two College Quizzes as part of this year's quizzing extravaganza. All quizzes will take place in room G21 of the Old Physics Building.

  • SciBizTech Quiz by Thejaswi Udupa
    Friday, 18th January at 3 PM
  • The General Quiz by Major Chandrakant Nair
    Saturday, 19th January at 9 AM
Essential Information
  • Teams of 3 or less.
  • Registration fee of 150 per team per quiz
  • First Prize 7.5k and Second Prize 5k
  • Cross-college teams are allowed.
Rules
  • Identity Cards should be produced upon request.
  • The Quiz Master will be the final arbitrator regarding all diputes.
  • Pravega reserves the right to disqualify any team not adhering to rules and regulations.
Rules
  • The quizzes are open only to students pursuing full-time UG/PG courses. Students pursuing MPhil and PhD courses are not allowed to take part.
  • No member representing any professional institute such as ICAI, ICSI, CFA etc., can participate (The list of professional courses is not exhaustive) and must be a full student of college.
  • No limit on the number of teams representing each college.
  • Any IISc student who fulfils all other criteria for participation are allowed to participate.

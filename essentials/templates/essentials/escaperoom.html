Escape Room

Pravega is proud to announce a brand new fun and challenging free-for-all event, combining intriguing clues with mind-bending puzzles that will push your creative thinking to its limits! It will be conducted on all three days (18th, 19th and 20th January) in the Tutorial room - 3 in the UG office

Description
  • Time limit: 20 minutes
  • Groups of 4 participants at a time.
  • Registration on the spot. Participants have to pay Rs 50 per team, and a special concession will be provided for school students.
  • Solve all puzzles in the time limit to win.
  • Get the wrong answer, or run out of time, and you lose.

Features a new puzzle each day!

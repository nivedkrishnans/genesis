BASE Science Quizzine

Pravega is proud to present BASE Science Quizzine, an inter-school science quiz. The quiz will set by the IISc UG Quiz Club and will be in a Prelims-Final format.

Venue: G21 Old Physics Building
Date: Saturday, 19th January, 2019
Time: 3 PM

Essential Information
  • Teams of 2 or less.
  • BASE Science Quizzine is an inter-school quiz. Both member of the team should be from the same school (same branch of a school if it has branches)
  • Registration is free.
  • First Prize 6k and Second Prize 4k
Format
  • The top eight teams will be selected into the finals through a written preliminary round.
  • The final will comprise of various rounds at the end of which, the winner will be declared.
  • All rules will be clarified at the event if a doubt arises.
Rules
  • Identity Cards should be produced upon request.
  • There is no cap on the number of teams that can participate from a school
  • Pravega reserves the right to disqualify any team not adhering to rules and regulations.
  • The Quiz Master will be the final arbitrator regarding all diputes.

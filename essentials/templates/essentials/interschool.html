BASE Inter-school Science Competition

This event aims to expose the students to the thrill of doing Science and to make them aware about the fact that they can follow their passion for Science by actually pursuing a career in Science.
The competition will test the scientific thinking skills of the students. The event also includes an inspirational talk by Prof. D.D. Sarma - an eminent Scientist from the Indian Institute of Science.

Event Details
Date: 18th of January, 2019
Venue: JN Tata Auditorium, Indian Institute of Science, CV Raman Road, Bengaluru
Schedule:
  1. 9:00 AM : The event begins
  2. 9:10 AM : The Prelims begin
  3. 10:15 AM : Talk by Prof. D.D. Sarma
  4. 11:15 AM : Refreshments Break
  5. 11:35 AM : Final round begins
  6. The event will conclude before 12:30 PM
Important information:
  • One school can nominate up to three teams.
  • Number of students per each team: 3 (at least two from classes 9th-10th and the third from classes 9th - 11th)
  • There is no participation fee.
  • One school teacher should preferably accompany the school team(s).

Wikimedia India Photography Contest

Pravega brings to you a very unique photography contest. You get to do what you love (Photography), contribute to free online open source content on Wikimedia Commons, and also earn while you do so! Earn a fully sponsored trip around Bangalore and stand a chance to win prizes worth 10,000!

Submit Now
How Do I Participate?
  • No restriction on age group, anyone with access to a device that can take images.
  • Create a user account on Wikimedia, upload your photos on Wikimedia Commons, and share the link(s) of the images in the submissions portal. How?
  • You can continue to submit photos till December 10th, 2018.
  • You can submit only five photos, but you can edit your submissions till 11:59 PM December 10th, 2018.
  • Top 20 submitters will be announced on 15th December 2018.
What Next?
